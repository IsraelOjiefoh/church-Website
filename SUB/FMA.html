In order to receive acceptance into Freedom Ministry Academy, you will need to complete our 3 step process. This process includes providing our team with some personal and academic information and making payment online.

  • Fill Application form

    • You can apply to Freedom Ministry Academy by clicking the button below. You will be directed to fill the application form. Please note that you have to select your preferred location to attend classes

  • Make Payment

    • After filling the registration form, you will be redirected to the payment modal where you will be required to make a payment of 5000. This can be done with any of the payment methods available.

    • After Step 1 and 2 are completed, you will be notified about your acceptance status. Kindly check your email regularly for updates. For further information kindly send an email to: freedomministryacademy@muyiwaareo.com or call +234 803 863 8402
